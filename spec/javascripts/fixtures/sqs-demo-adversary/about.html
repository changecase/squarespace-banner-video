Adversary is an experienced DJ and producer in the New York electronic music scene.

 

A fixture in the New York DJ community for over 10 years, Adversary currently works as a house DJ at Cascade Bar in Prospect Heights, Brooklyn. Perhaps most famous for striking gold in 2008 with his remix of Opal Siren's hit single "Meltdown," he is a veteran with a wide variety of experience, having played nightclubs, weddings, private events, festivals, and produced original tracks and remixes.
