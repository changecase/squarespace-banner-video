A stylish go-anywhere, haul-anything utility bike.

 
 
 

The Fulton Cycle is exactly the bike you need, at precisely the moment when you need it.

 

The Fulton Cycle's quick-connect mounting system and modular accessories allow you to create the bike that you need on the fly, without breaking out the wrench. With a functional 3D-printed steel frame and a built-in cable lock and lighting, the Fulton Cycle is the only bike you'll ever need for city life.
